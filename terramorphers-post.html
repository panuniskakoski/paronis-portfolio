About the project

We (as Unibyte Studios) have been working on Terramorphers since summer of 2019.

Game is a tactical rpg game for Android devices.

I have done all the graphical elements related to the project, including all the marketing material and UI elements. I hold it as a center piece of my portfolio.

I have done over 150 character designs for the game. Character design is also one of my favourite tasks regarding game development. Here is a quick overview of the game.

The best way to see all the work I've done on the game is to simply play it. Link to Play Store below. More assets and art of the game will be uploaded to my ArtStation account.

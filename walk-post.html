A walk in the park

About the project

Completely solo project (2021). Project took around 50h of work. All assets, audio and programming is my work. Learning a lot while doing.

The idea is that you play as a man trying to maintain his relationship as you are taking a walk in the park with your girlfriend. Read the games tutorial page from main menu.

The game is short and kind of a meme game for Android devices.

I got this dumb game idea about a year a go and luckyly got a chance to execute it later as a school project.

The best way to see all the work I've done on the game is to simply play it. Download the apk from my Google Drive. Link below. I added also link to the projects GitHub repository.

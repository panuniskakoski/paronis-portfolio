About the project

This was my very first game prototype! A silly solo project from 2018. Programming, audio and graphics are made by me. Can't recall how much time this took me. Was mainly just fooling around with Aseprite (pixel art tool) and Game Maker Studio 2. Trying to learn how GMS works and how to produce pixel art animations and tile maps.

The terrain is made with tile maps. Game logic is written with GMS own language.

Sup.

My name is Panu 'paroni' Niskakoski and I'm a mixed-media artist and a game developer from Finland.

I have over 6 years of work experience in game development both as an entrepreneur and as an employ.

I also do all sorts of mixed-media work as a freelancer and I have a master's degree in Information Technology.

Welcome to my portfolio page.

TOP EXPERTISE

In essence I am a generalist. My main thing is 2D art, but I've done plenty of 3D as well. I do level & game design (docs + spreadsheets included), sound production, video editing and particle effects. I can help with config stuff and I know some programming. Aaaand I'm always the one who plays the game the most and knows the project inside out. So testing and QA also.

Through entrepreneurship and my work as a CEO in my own company, I've also learned a thing or two about business. Though I hate all the corporate bureaucracy — bleh. In a beautiful world one could just focus on the development.

About me

I am an IT student from University of Jyväskylä and will be graduating most likely at the end of the year 2021.

My main expertise is 2D graphics in general, but I am also very interested in animation, audio (SFX, music, voice acting) and storytelling. 3D modelling is also something I am planning to tip my toes in the near future.

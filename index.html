Hello.

My name is Panu Niskakoski
and I am an IT graduate and
an entrepreneur from Finland.

I am a fresh IT graduate from University of Jyväskylä. On my I studies specialized on game development and I did my masters thesis about animal-computer interaction (ACI).

Right now I am a game artist at Unibyte Studios (since summer of 2019). I provide all the graphical bits and pieces from game assets to marketing material. I also produce game music and SFX.

In addition I am a freelance artist. I have made little bit of everything from band shirt and skateboard designs to company logos and social media postings.


Top expertise

My main expertise is 2D graphics in general, but I have also experience in animation, audio (SFX, composing, voice acting) and storytelling. I have quite a lot of experience on testing and reporting. 3D-modeling is also on my bucket list.

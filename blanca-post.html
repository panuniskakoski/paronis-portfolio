About the project

Completely solo project (2019). I can't recall exactly, but this project took around 30h of work(?). Mostly on the pixel art modelling. All visual assets, audio and programming is my work.

The idea was just to model our apartment with pixel art graphics. Made the application with Game Maker Studio 2.

I was mostly focusing on learning pixel art.

Chicken Manager

About the project

My first game jam project. Everything was done within 48h weekend. Our team was formed of three people. I provided the graphics and animation. Also participated on planning and design with others.

In the game you play as a chicken farmer who hast to collect and sell as many eggs as he can. With money you can buy more chicken. You can also buy cages for the chicken, so they won't lay their eggs all around.

The game is just a prototype and it would be neat to take this concept further. I made a bunch of unused assets and animations that didn't make it into the jam proto.

The best way to see all the work I've done on the game is to simply play it. You can play it on a browser (keyboard required) at Itch.io. Link below.

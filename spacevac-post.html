Space Vac

About the project

This team project was part of a school course. We got to design a game with a team. I worked as a game artist.

The idea was to fly around vacuuming space critters and smashing them into pieces with your plasma ball chamber like pinball and collecting the resources they'd drop. With the resources you could upgrade your ship and travel farther in space. The idea was neat, but we should have discussed more about the game design. Anyway, I still liked the characters and animations I made for the game.

The game is still downloadable in Play Store. Some of the character animations are available on my Instagram page in the saved stories -section.

The best way to see all the work I've done on the game is to simply play it in VR. You can't see all the little particles and such on the screen. Download the apk from my Google Drive. Link below. I added also link to the projects GitHub repository.

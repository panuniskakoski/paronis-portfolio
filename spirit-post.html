We (as Unibyte Studios) have been working on Spirit Trials since fall of 2022.

This game is simpler and much more casual compared to Terramorphers.

I have done all the graphical elements related to the project, including all the marketing material and UI icons/images (excluding particle effects and UI panels and buttons).

But unlike with Terramorphers I composed, recorded and mixed the soundtrack of the game. I also recorded and edited many of the SFX including all the boss enemy sounds.

I made the trailer below as well.

The best way to see all the work I've done on the game is to simply play it. Link to Play Store below. More assets and art of the game will be uploaded to my ArtStation account.

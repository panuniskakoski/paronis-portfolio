We (as in Unibyte Studios) have been working on Spirit Trials since fall of 2022.

The game is more casual roguelite bullet hell rpg for mobile.

I have done all the graphical elements related to the project, including all the marketing material / Play Store images and UI icons/images (excluding particle effects and UI panels and buttons).

I composed, recorded and mixed the soundtrack of the game. I also recorded and edited many of the SFX including all the boss enemy sounds.
